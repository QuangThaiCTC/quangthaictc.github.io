Skills

Information Gathering

Information gathering is the initial phase of ethical hacking, where experts collect data on the target system, uncovering potential vulnerabilities.

Vulnerability Assessment

Vulnerability assessment is a systematic process where security professionals identify, classify, and prioritize weaknesses in a system to mitigate potential threats effectively.

Exploitation

Exploitation is the phase in cybersecurity where attackers take advantage of identified vulnerabilities to gain unauthorized access, control, or information from the target system.

Lateral Movement

Lateral movement is a technique used by attackers after initial access, where they navigate through a network, escalating privileges and accessing other systems to expand control or gather sensitive data.
